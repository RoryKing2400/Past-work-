3.6¶

From the boxplot we can tell that there is a big disparity in the total profits earned by our top selling products and our worst selling products. As a company we want all of our products to be hitting our goal for each quarter, and if we can pick our bottom few prodcuts then all of our products will be reaching our goal. We should look into increasing the advertising of our beverages, fruits and personal care products. If the sale profits of these don't increase with our advertisment efforts we will have to look into cutting those prodcuts out of our product list.
